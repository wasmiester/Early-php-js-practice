Gosselin Motors, one of the world's largest automakers, was founded in 1911, and today manufactures cars and trucks in 23 countries. With its global headquarters in San Francisco, Gosselin Motors employs more than 100,000 people in every major region of the world, and sells and services vehicles in over 80 countries.

Our company goal is to manufacture automobiles that are of the highest quality, affordable, and most importantly, safe. Plus, as an environmental leader, Gosselin Motors does more than meet industry standards-we seek to raise them. With an unwavering commitment to environmental protection, Gosselin Motors strives to create clean and efficient products, and to conserve resources before our vehicles even hit the road.

At Gosselin Motors, we believe continued growth toward a more inclusive culture is necessary for business success. Our customers, business partners and employees should see themselves represented in our workforce, in our marketing campaigns, in our dealerships, in the communities we serve, and the organizations we support.
